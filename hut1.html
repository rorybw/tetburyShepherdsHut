The Location

Our cosy renovated Cotswold shepherd's hut is surrounded by nothing but fields and wildlife. No road noise - perfect peace .... located in the heart of the countryside but close to the local village of Cherington and the historic market town of Tetbury. 12 miles from Cirencester, the Capital of the Cotswolds.
The shepherd's hut is in a paddock very near the main house (B & B separately listed), with a cosy 4' double bed, a small stove and candles for overnight illumination - plus electricity if needed for a radiator and an electric blanket ..... woo hoo ........

Facilities

Guests can use the bathroom (power shower, bath & WC), in the main house, which is a converted barn, there is also an eco loo close by the hut. Guests are invited to come into the cosy warm dining room for a cooked or continental breakfast. We always use local produce, organic where possible. Guests are free to enjoy our garden and paddock. There is wireless internet access in and near the house, but the shepherd's hut is probably not close enough .... might be a good thing if you want some isolation!
