Your Location
Zoom out to see nothing but fields

There are wonderful walks all around us down the green lanes into the valley at Nags Head and then along to a beautiful wild lake at Cherington with wild duck, swans and moorhens. You can walk through Gatcombe woods, carpeted with bluebells in the spring.

We are 10 minutes’ drive from Westonbirt Arboretum, Chavanage and the famous “Jolly Nice” café. Tetbury is a 5-minute drive, full of antique shops and delicious cafes. Berkeley Castle, a Medieval Castle, belonging to the Berkeley family and The Slimbridge Wildfowl and Wetlands Trust is a half hour drive away.
