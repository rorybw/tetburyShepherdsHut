Location

The shepherds huts are 4 miles outside Tetbury located in rolling fields, near the lovely, Best Kept Village winner, Cherington. There are wonderful walks all around us down the green lanes into the valley at Nags Head and then along to a beautiful wild lake at Cherington with wild duck, swans and moorhens. You can walk through Gatcombe woods, carpeted with bluebells in the spring.

Tetbury is a 5 minute drive, full of antique shops and delicious cafes. We are 10 minutes’ drive from Westonbirt Arboretum, Chavanage and the famous “Jolly Nice” café. There are excellent local pubs, great local cuisine and an award-winning farmers market in Stroud, 15 mins away. Berkeley Castle, a medieval castle, and The Slimbridge Wildfowl and Wetlands Trust is a half hour drive away.

We are within a 30-40 minutes drive of the beautiful Georgian City of Bath, Cheltenham Spa, Gloucester and Bristol.

The address of the shepherds huts is Lockstone Bottom Barn, Cherington, Tetbury, Gloucestershire, GL8 8PF. Please note the post code can cause problems with some older sat-navs, so check that the destination is about a mile outside Cherington before setting off. Please also note there are many unnamed country lanes around the property so be sure to check the location online (Google Maps) to get an idea off the property's whereabouts.

Coming from London the best route is to follow the M4 to Swindon, the A419 to Cirencester and then A429 (changes into A433) to Tetbury.

Coming from the North the best route is to follow the M5 to Stroud, the A46 to Nailsworth, Avening Road to Avening and then Star Lane to the property.

Coming from the South West the best route is to follow the M5 to Bristol, the M4 to junction 18, the A46 towards Nailsworth, Avening Road to Avening and then Star Lane to the property.
