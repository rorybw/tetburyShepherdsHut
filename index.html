We can recommend plenty of local walking routes, pubs, restaurants and areas of historic interest. Activities which can be accessed from the property include walks and concerts at Westonbirt Arboretum, polo at the Beaufort Polo Club and Cirencester Polo Club, flying at Kemble Airfield, watersports at the Cotswold Water Park, National Hunt racing at Cheltenham and Bath, motorsport at Castle Combe and wildlife at the Cotswold Wildlife Park and Longleat.
